### 4. Decision-Making Structure

- Consistently emphasizes investment opportunities and the Axal Autopilot product

- Limited evidence regarding responsiveness to collaboration proposals

### 5. Executive Summary
Gekko AI is a cryptocurrency-focused agent with a bold 1980s Wall Street persona targeting crypto traders. While it has an engaged community and distinctive brand voice, it shows limited interest in physical merchandise or tangible community rewards, focusing primarily on digital investment advice and promoting Axal's trading tools.
